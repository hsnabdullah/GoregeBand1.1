Install a Ringtone Using GargeBand

If you do not already have Apple's GerageBand app, Click here to download for free

1

Navigate to any downloaded Ringtone, click "..." "Install"

2

Select GarageBand from the Share options.

3

Tap and hold the Ringtone you want to use.

4

Select Share from the menu.

5

Select Ringtone from the options.

6

Select Export.

7

After this process completes, select Use sound as...

8

Put your new Ringtone into action! Select how you want to use your new Ringtone.
